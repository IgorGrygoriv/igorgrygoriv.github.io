The BMW E90 M3, produced between 2007 and 2013, is a legendary sports sedan known for its exceptional balance of performance and everyday usability. It features a naturally aspirated 4.0-liter V8 engine, capable of delivering 414 horsepower and revving up to an impressive 8,400 RPM, making it one of the most exhilarating engines in the M3 lineage. Its precise handling, rear-wheel-drive layout, and advanced suspension system provide an engaging driving experience, while its refined interior and four-door practicality offer comfort for daily use. The E90 M3 has since become a highly sought-after model for enthusiasts, embodying the peak of BMW's M3 legacy.
